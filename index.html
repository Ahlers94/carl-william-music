About Me

Musicians aren't duplicating my rig for a straightforward reason: putting it together requires skills across three distinct technical disciplines. To build what I have, a person must simultaneously act as an audio engineer, a hardware systems builder, and a software developer. The vast majority of guitarists want plug-and-play simplicity. I want to explore sonic possibilities.
